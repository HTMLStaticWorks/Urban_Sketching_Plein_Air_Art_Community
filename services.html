The Sketching Protocol

Experience the joy of outdoor painting with our structured meetup protocol. We guide you from finding the perfect spot to finalizing and sharing your artwork with a supportive community.

01. Location Scouting

Finding shaded, safe areas with great sightlines for the whole group to set up comfortably.

02. Composition Planning

Discussing focal points, framing, and thumbnail sketches before starting the main piece.

03. Group Throw-Down

A supportive final review where everyone lays their sketchbooks down to share their work.
