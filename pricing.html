No! We recommend starting with the Minimalist Kit (a sketchbook and pen/pencil). You can always add watercolors and specialized tools later as you discover your personal style.

We suggest high-quality student-grade materials for beginners (like Winsor & Newton Cotman for paints), but heavily encourage investing in 100% cotton paper for the best painting experience.

You can put together an excellent beginner kit for under $50. The core essentials are just a sketchbook, a pen, and a basic travel watercolor set. Expensive tools won't make you a better artist!

A5 (roughly 5.8 x 8.3 inches) is the golden standard. It fits easily into almost any bag, is comfortable to hold in one hand while standing, and offers enough space for expansive panoramic scenes.
