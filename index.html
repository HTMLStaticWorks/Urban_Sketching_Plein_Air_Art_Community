How It Works

Your journey into the world of urban sketching.

1

1. Find a Chapter

Locate a local urban sketching chapter or meetup happening in your city.

2

2. Pack Your Gear

Grab your sketchbook, a few pens, and a small watercolor kit. Keep it light and mobile.

3

3. Sketch on Location

Spend a couple of hours observing and drawing your environment alongside other artists.

4

4. The Throwdown

Gather at the end of the meetup to share your work, receive encouragement, and take a group photo.
